Spaghetti Carbonara

A creamy Italian pasta dish with pancetta, eggs, cheese, and pepper.

  1. Boil pasta in salted water.
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy.
  3. Mix eggs with cheese.
  4. Combine everything and serve hot.
